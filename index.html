My Daily Targets

Your personal diary

My Daily Targets uses local storage on your browser, so your information is completely safe, visible to only you. It is not saved on a cloud, on your Facebook account or anywhere else,

Since it is saved on your browser and not through an account, note that if you go on a different device or browser, your daily targets will not be displayed.

There will soon be a facility to look through your targets history through a calendar.
